# Daima — Pitch for Judges

## One-liner

**The AI CFO that helps remote workers in emerging economies keep more of what they earn.**

## The Story

Daniela is a UX designer in Mexico City. She's been working remotely for US companies for 4 years. She's talented, reliable, and consistently delivers great work.

She's also losing thousands of dollars every year — and she doesn't know it.

- She pays 5-8% in fees every time she gets paid through PayPal
- She charges $28/hour — the market rate for her profile is $42-55/hour
- She has no financial strategy — her savings sit in pesos losing value to inflation
- No accountant, no CFO, no one looking out for her financial wellbeing

**Every fintech platform today was built for the employer.** Deel, Remote, Papaya Global — they solve payroll compliance for companies. Nobody built the financial agent that sits on Daniela's side.

## What Daima Does

Daima is Daniela's AI CFO:

1. **Receives payments instantly** — USDC on Solana via OWS, near-zero fees
2. **Auto-distributes** — 50% expenses (USDC), 40% savings (BTC via Lightning), 10% investments
3. **Knows her market value** — flags offers below market rate and counter-offers automatically
4. **Grows her wealth** — moves savings to Bitcoin, building real purchasing power

For companies: **Fair Pay Badge** — proof you pay within market range. Real employer branding.

## Why This Matters

- **$674B** gig economy, growing to $2.1T by 2034
- **50-100M** remote workers in emerging economies
- **$7,200/year** lost per worker to payment fees alone
- **77%** of LATAM companies reported late payments in 2025

## OWS Integration

- OWS manages the multi-chain wallet (Solana + Bitcoin)
- Worker gets one wallet, multiple chains — abstracted away
- Company pays to one address — Daima handles the rest
- Future: x402 for agent-to-agent micropayments
